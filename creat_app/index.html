Why Build Business Credit?

Business credit is separate from your personal credit and is crucial for securing loans, lines of credit, and favorable terms with suppliers. A strong business credit profile can help you:

  • Access larger amounts of capital
  • Obtain lower interest rates
  • Improve cash flow
  • Negotiate better payment terms
  • Protect your personal assets
